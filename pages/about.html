About Ailurus Learning

Our mission is to help provide educational resources to help users learn and grow in the field of Computer Science, specifically lower division topics. To accomplish this we created brief and interactive lessons that make learning enjoyable and accessible to everyone. To promote consistent learning habits we have implemented habit tracking features and automated email notifications that remind users to study to complete their daily lesson so users do not lose their study streak.
